title: Louisville
---
Formed in 1943, Louisville makes a number of premium baseball sporting
products; mostly known for their Louisville slugger, a hit on the market known for
its consistent quality and history of use by major baseball stars in the United
States. The company stocks have tanked, but revived around the 2010s alter an
explosion in baseball's popularity as a pastime and the national sport of Urssea.

## Products:
- Louisville Slugger
- Titanium Core (Slugger Series T)  

    Notable User: Sabujin Sabileye
-  SynPitch Leather Pitching Gloves
- Exclusive MLB Competition Grade Baseballs
