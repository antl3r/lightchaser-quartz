title: FMM

Fuck your Motherfucking Manufacturing (often shortened down to FMM) is a legally registered limited industry company in Urssea, supposedly formed around 2032. It is operated by a single company head, who remains unidentified under the alias "Uzukh Maiddek". They allegedly 	accept custom steel working and maintenance contracts for exorbitant prices and acquire materials through dubious means. Their company value is unknown and do not seem to place themselves in any given centralized location.

# Known Products:
- KT-16A, "Judas Iscariot" + KT-16B, "Jesus Scabbard"

    A set of modern katana and auto-sharpening scabbard that utilizes the vibration generated by user movement to operate a ratcheting angled whetstone mechanism. Commissioned by a private client for MK3,000
- "Mesin KO"

     A seemingly standard Louisville Slugger T-1 decorated in various permanent marker ink sketches. It weighs 2 Kg, the wooden furbish on the surface seems to be a cover up for a metallic core. It generates up to 10,000 - 11,000 pounds of force on impact. Commissioned by a private client for MK3,000
