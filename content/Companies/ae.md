---
title: Advanced Electronics
---
Advanced Electronics (often referred to as AE) is a manufacturer of cybernetic control units (CCU), neuro-compatible CPUs, NP10-3.0 expansion cards for interacting with high-performance cybernetics, among other cybernetic and desktop computing products. Their more broad catalog of items is often praised as one of the reasons they can keep costs down but has been accused of outsourcing productions to the Angel Industrial Complex.

## Products:
- AE Code Red, Gen. 1 - 10 neuro-compatible CPUs.
- AE MOD-1 to MOD-10 CCUs.

    - Comes in variants, denoted by a letter suffix (M, A, F, GF).
- AE DreamerGL

    - A graphics library capable of running high resolution 3D graphics on an Eyes Up Display (EUD) optic. The main graphics engine of many VR games.
- AE SM

    - Classified.
- AEDisplay

    - An interfacing standard for outputting audiovisual signals to a
monitor or otherwise visual output device from a CCU.
