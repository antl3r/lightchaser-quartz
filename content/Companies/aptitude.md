---
title: Aptitude
---
Aptitude is a USA manufacturing company mostly comprising their product line of cybernetic control unit (CCU) expansion boards running their integrated proprietary 10100/E/F/X - 13900/E/F/X architecture CPUs. They are the lead in the battle for consumer cybernetic control unit sales against Advanced Electronics by their models running an average 4% faster and compatibility with proprietary feature drivers to expand on certain products such as Yujian HiColor.

Products:
- Aptitude 10, 11, 12, and 13 series neuro-compatible CPUs.
- Aptitude NX (NeuroXpander) series CCUs

    - Marketed towards consumers looking for high-end performance.
- Aptitude Nucleus series CCUs.

    - Standard CCUs marketed towards the low to middle-end consumer.
- Aptitude SC-11187-00001 to SC-11189-90000 series CCUs.

    - Classified, limited use military CCUs to allow for interfacing with remote control drones.

   - SC stands for Soul Capture.
- NP 1.0-3.0
    
    - Standard specifications of a Neuro Port. Formerly proprietary standard. Used widely.
