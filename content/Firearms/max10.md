# Origin
- The Lage MAX-10 is a detachable, customizable upper receiver.
- Handmade by John as a proposal gift for Sabujin. Sabujin discovered it unfinished in John’s drawer while John was in a coma.
-  The gun was engraved manually on a lathe with: “come home”

# Mechanics
- The MAX-10 upper can be configured as
    - Pistol carbine
    - Submachine gun
- Retrofitted with slots for electronic components.
- Includes laser sights.
- Smart-linked to Sabujin’s biometrics.
- The firing mechanism locking password is: “some day.”

# Notes
- "Come home" references When You Come Home - Brian Immanugel.
- “Some day” references We’ll Meet Again — “some sunny day.”
- It is modern and modular, contrasting the VAG-73’s antiquated experimental design.
